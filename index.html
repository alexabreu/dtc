DownEast Treatment Center

Struggling with your use of opioids?

Medication assisted treatment at the DownEast Treatment Center may be for you.

Our program is now more client-centered and flexible than ever. Our team will work with you to create a treatment plan that works for you and your schedule. With evening hours and customized treatment, we can help you succeed in recovery.

Free services available. Customized approach. Confidential. Respectful. Compassionate.

The DownEast Treatment Center is the hub of a "hub and spoke" treatment model for substance use conditions developed by the DownEast Treatment Network partnership. We offer comprehensive plans, including medication and counseling, for treatment of substance use conditions, including opioid use disorder. We offer help and hope to people struggling with chemical dependency, addiction, or substance misuse.
